Hi. My name is Gordon Koo. I'm a software developer in the San Francisco Bay Area, currently working at LinkedIn, a site aimed at connecting professionals. Here is my LinkedIn profile.

In my freshman year of college, my dorm resident assistant put a guitar in my hands and taught me a handful of chords and the basics of strumming. Since then, I've become quite fond of playing guitar (and more recently, ukulele) and it's my most frequent and enjoyed hobby today.

I like to work on websites. I suppose that's a good trait to have for someone in my profession. My proclivity to work on personal projects outside of work started with my discovery of LinkedIn's Hackdays, an internal event hosted every month where employees are encouraged to work on side projects and learn about new technologies.

This site was made using Jekyll, Backbone, Less, and some random CSS3.
