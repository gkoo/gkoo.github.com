projects

  • Connection Timeline

    The Connection Timeline is an interactive visualization of the LinkedIn connections you've made throughout your career. I worked on this project as part of LinkedIn's hackday in April 2011. It is currently featured on LinkedIn Labs.

  • Ready, Set, Art!

    An experimental pictionary game I wrote with NodeJS, Socket.IO, and Backbone.

  • Khan Academy Roulette

    A viewer for watching Khan Academy videos. When I first visited the Khan Academy website, I was overwhelmed by the huge library of videos on the Khan Academy site. I had the idea to create a random video viewer for the library using their API. This is the result.

  • Coverflow

    One of the new features in Firefox 10 was support for CSS 3D transforms. Now that both Firefox and Chrome (and Safari, I suppose) support 3D transforms, I decided to kick the tires and make an in-browser implementation of coverflow, the popular visualization by Apple.
